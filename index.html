Monetizing your website shouldn't make you feel guilty.

Placeable is an open source, content-focused ad network that doesn't compromise your users, like other big ad networks.

Placeable doesn't compromise your user's data. The belief behind Placeable ads, is to target ads based on the content of a website, not based on the browsing habits of its users.

Placeable is an open source. Anyone can read its source code, anyone can contribute to it and anyone can verify that it is actually, what it's advertised as.

Placeable is lightning-fast. It is built using modern technologies and saves storage and bandwidth by being largely text-based. That also means it doesn't really interfere with the load times of your website.
